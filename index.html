Mystic TV: Surprise

Korea's longest-running and most popular dramatised entertainment show aired on MBC TV

It is an omnibus drama that revolves around extraordinary or supernatural events that have occurred all over the world. The show has been airing for 20 years, making it one of the longest-running programs in South Korea and a symbol of dramatised content. Over the course of two decades, the show has helped launch the careers of several top-level actors and directors. Despite its long run, the show still continues to captivate audiences and is beloved by many.

  • Video: Sample clip of the show
    • Role: Directing, Editing
  • Achievement: Top 20 ratings in Korea, Won the best programme award from MBC
